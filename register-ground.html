Whether you are a freelancer looking for a team, an amateur team looking to have a shot at the top of the podium and the prize or a recognized team in the Patras residency, looking for healthy sportsmanship and fierce competition, sign up now, and join the tournament.
Below, you may choose to register as a manager or as user/player.

Sign up as a Team Manager




Team Manager

Signing up as a team manager, means that you are managing a team that officialy belongs to the footbal organisation and that you can prove it with the proper paperwork. Not long after, the administrator will approve of your authentication and your team can make it into the competitors list.

    As the Team Manager

  • You can create your own team
  • Manage your draft and positions in full scale
  • Change your team composition at any given time, for up to few days before your team's matches

Sign up as Player/User




Registered User


    Registering allows you to:

  • Apply to be drafted to a team
  • Set up and make changes to your summary
  • Use the stake feature, where you can bet on teams and players, recieving profits and ultimately supporting the teams and the tournament above and beyond prizepool and sponsors respectively
